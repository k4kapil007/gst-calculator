GST Rate Categories (India)

  • 0%: Essential goods (milk, bread, educational services)
  • 5%: Essential items (spices, coal, life-saving drugs)
  • 12%: Standard rate items (computers, processed food)
  • 18%: Standard rate (hair oil, toothpaste, capital goods)
  • 28%: Luxury and demerit goods (cars, tobacco, cement)
